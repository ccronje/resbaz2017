--- layout: site permalink: sydney location: Sydney dates: 3rd - 5th July, 2017 splashImage: /img/sydney.jpg map: sites/sydney.geojson about: The Research Bazaar is an event promoting the digital literacy emerging at the center of modern research. ResBaz Sydney this year will be held at the University of Technology Sydney in July.

At ResBaz researchers come together to up-skill in next generation digital research tools and scholarship. In the spirit of a marketplace or bazaar, ResBaz is a highly participatory event where researchers from many different disciplines can learn, share knowledge and skills, and have fun!

Who should attend? Researchers at ALL levels from ALL disciplines. PhD and research Masters students and early career researchers are especially encouraged to apply. Research technologists who support researchers to do their job should also come along!

You don't have to be a student or staff member at UTS, ResBaz is open to anyone involved in research who wants to learn new skills and make new connections! schedule: The Research Bazaar will start with two days of workshops on July 3rd and 4th. You can choose to sign-up for a 2-day workshop to learn R or Python, or you can mix and match shorter workshops over the two days.

The third day is the ResBaz Festival Day, which will include lightning talks and researcher led workshops as well as lots of opportunities to network and learn more about your fellow researchers.

All three days will be full of social events and networking opportunities, so get ready to broaden your horizons and extend your personal research community! days: - day: Day 1 (July 3) sessions: - title: Programming with Python (2 days) id: scpy1 blurb: Day one of a two day workshop introducing you to the programming language Python with lots of hands-on exercises. R enables a wide range of statistical analyses and data visualisations, including text and data mining, and is used by researchers in all disciplines, from science and maths to social science and humanities. - title: Cleaning and Exploring your Data with Open Refine (half day - morning) id: openrefine blurb: Open Refine is a powerful, free tool for exploring, normalising and cleaning datasets, and extending data by accessing the internet through APIs. Scraping is an incredibly useful for acquiring data where it has been made inaccessible by the way it’s presented online. But as well as acquiring data, scraping can be really useful for other things like tracking changes and archiving.
